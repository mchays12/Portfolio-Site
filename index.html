About Me

My name is Matthew Hays and I am a full-stack web developer based in Traverse City, Mi and Brooklyn, NY. I specialize in creating dynamic and responsive web applications using technologies such as React, Node.js, Angular, Typescript, and more. I strive to create user-centric real world solutions. My experience in these languages, and my understanding of backend databases, such as MongoDB and PostgreSQL, allow me to develop comprehensive full-stack applications. In addition to these skills, I have a background in mechanical engineering, run a successful summertime business, and have an MBA. I have a strong desire for continious learning, collaboration, and creative but practical solutions.


Outside of coding, I enjoy writing music, working out, hiking, snowboarding, and cycling. If it requires me to be outdoors, I'm there! Please feel free to explore my portfolio to see my work or contact me for collaborations or job opportunities.
